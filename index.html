Jiaer Xia

Hi! I'm Jiaer Xia, a second-year PhD student at the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University, supervised by Prof. Kaiyang Zhou.

Previously, I was a Master student at the MACLab at Xiamen University, supervised by Prof. Rongrong Ji. I received my Bachelor's Degree in Engineering (2017-2021) from the Donghua University, working closely with Prof. Mingbo Zhao.

My current research mainly focuses on Multimodal LLMs. Feel free to contact me.
